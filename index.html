WHAT I DO

Premium Cocoa Bean Sourcing & Procurement

CHOCOCAM SARL Ltd specializes in the sourcing and procurement of high-quality cocoa beans directly from trusted farmers and certified cooperatives. We ensure strict selection standards, traceability, and quality control throughout the sourcing process. Our strong relationships with local producers allow us to maintain consistent supply volumes while supporting sustainable agricultural practices and ethical sourcing principles.

Quality Control, Processing & Packaging

We implement rigorous quality assurance procedures to meet international export standards. Cocoa beans undergo professional sorting, grading, and inspection to ensure compliance with global market requirements. Our processing and packaging solutions are designed to preserve freshness, maintain product integrity, and meet buyer specifications, including customized packaging options for international shipments.

Export Logistics & International Documentation

CHOCOCAM SARL Ltd provides complete export logistics management, ensuring smooth and efficient delivery to global destinations. Our services include handling export documentation, customs clearance, freight coordination, and compliance with international trade regulations. We work with trusted logistics partners to guarantee secure, timely shipments and transparent communication throughout the supply chain.

Global Trade Partnerships & Client Support

We build long-term partnerships with international buyers by offering reliable communication, transparent transactions, and flexible supply solutions. Our dedicated export team supports clients from initial inquiry through final delivery, providing market insights, contract management, and tailored solutions to meet specific sourcing requirements.

FEATURED CATEGORIES

Premium Cocoa Beans

High-quality raw cocoa beans sourced directly from trusted farmers and cooperatives. Our premium beans are carefully selected, fermented, and dried according to international standards, ensuring consistent flavor profiles and quality suitable for chocolate manufacturers and global processors.

Fermented & Dried Cocoa

Professionally fermented and sun-dried cocoa beans prepared to meet export-grade specifications. This category focuses on maintaining optimal moisture levels, purity, and uniformity to satisfy international quality requirements and enhance product value.

Bulk Export Cocoa Supplies

Large-volume cocoa shipments designed for international buyers, traders, and manufacturers. We provide reliable bulk supply solutions with flexible packaging options, efficient logistics coordination, and consistent product availability.

QUESTIONS

01. What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our MOQ is one 20ft container, approximately 19-20 metric tonnes.

02. Which ports do you ship from?

We export from Douala Port and Kribi Deep Seaport depending on shipment requirements.

03. What documentation is provided with shipments?

Phytosanitary certificate, certificate of origin, quality analysis, and export clearance documents.

04. What payment terms do you accept?

We accept TT, LC, and negotiated terms for long-term partners.

05. Can you provide samples?

Yes, samples are available for qualified buyers. Shipping costs depend on destination.
