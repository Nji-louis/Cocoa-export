Frequently Asked Questions

01. What is your minimum order quantity for bulk cocoa beans?

Our MOQ is one 20ft container, approximately 19-20 metric tonnes of export-ready cocoa beans, depending on product type and shipment requirements.

02. Which ports do you use for international cocoa shipping?

We export from Douala Port and Kribi Deep Seaport depending on the buyer destination, container routing, freight availability, and shipment schedule.

03. What export documents are provided with cocoa shipments?

Shipments can include commercial invoice, packing list, phytosanitary certificate, certificate of origin, quality analysis, export clearance documents, and logistics records.

04. Can you support cocoa sourcing for long-term contracts?

Yes. COCOABRIDGE supports qualified buyers with recurring cocoa sourcing, contract planning, lot preparation, and negotiated commercial terms such as TT or LC.

05. Can qualified buyers receive cocoa samples before bulk supply?

Yes. Samples are available for qualified buyers before bulk cocoa supply discussions. Sample availability, courier cost, and documentation depend on the destination country.
