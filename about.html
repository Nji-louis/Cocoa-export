COCOABRIDGE Ltd

We are an export-focused cocoa company based in Douala, Cameroon, delivering traceable supply and dependable logistics to global buyers.

Our Mission

Deliver export-grade cocoa through responsible sourcing, strict quality standards, and efficient logistics for long-term buyer partnerships.

Our Vision

Be a trusted global cocoa export partner known for reliability, consistent quality, and support for sustainable growth in Cameroon.

Our Goal

Build a strong international presence by delivering consistent cocoa quality, dependable supply chains, and responsive customer service.

WHAT WE DO

Premium Cocoa Bean Sourcing & Procurement

We source export-grade cocoa from vetted farmers and cooperatives, with strict lot selection, traceability, and stable supply volumes for contract buyers.

Quality Control, Processing & Packaging

Each lot is sorted, graded, and inspected to international standards, then packed to buyer specifications to protect quality through transit.

Export Logistics & International Documentation

We handle export documentation, customs clearance, and freight coordination end to end for compliant and on-time international delivery.

Global Trade Partnerships & Client Support

Our team supports buyers from first inquiry to final shipment with transparent communication, flexible sourcing, and dependable execution.
