## Meta Description Strategy

- Keep descriptions between roughly 140 and 160 characters where practical.
- Lead with the commodity and Cameroon origin.
- Include buyer value: traceability, quality control, export documentation, logistics, samples or bulk supply.
- Avoid unsupported claims such as guaranteed certification, guaranteed rankings or guaranteed lowest prices.
